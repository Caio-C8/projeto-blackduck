Workouts da Semana

Segunda-Feira

Complete as many reps as possible in 10 minutes of:

10 Clean and jerk, Weight 1

Rest 1 minute

10 Clean and jerk, Weight 2

Rest 1 minute

10 Clean and jerk, Weight 3

Rest 1 minute

Max-reps Clean and jerk in time remaining, Weight 4.

♀ 85/125/155/165 lbs

♂ 135/185/225/245 lbs

Terça-Feira

3 rounds in 15 minutes of:

75 Double-under

15/21 Calorie Echo bike

5 Front Squat

♀ 155 lbs

♂ 225 lbs

Quarta-Feira

During 15 Minutes:

5 rounds of:

10 Thruster, Weight 1

10 Chest-to-bar Pull-up

Rest 1 minute, then:

5 rounds of:

7 Thruster, Weight 2

7 Bar muscle-up

Time cap: 15 minutes

♀ Weight 1: 65, Weight 2: 95 lbs

♂ Weight 1: 95, Weight 2: 135 lbs

Quinta-Feira

4 rounds for max reps:

1 minute of Snatches

1 minute of Rowing for Calories

1 minute of Dumbbell Box Step-ups

1 minute of rest

♀ 85 lbs barbell, 35 lbs dumbbells to a 50cm box

♂ 135 lbs barbell, 50 lbs dumbbells to a 50cm box

Sexta-Feira

3 rounds in 25 minutes:

50 Wall-ball Shots

50 Lateral Burpee Box Jump-overs

♀ 14 lbs ball to a 2.5m target, 50cm box

♂ 20 lbs ball to a 3m target, 60cm box

Sábado

During 22 minutes:

3 rounds:

10 Handstand Push-up

20 Toes-to-bar

2 rounds:

10 Strict Handstand Push-up

5 Rope climb (4.5m)

1 round:

10 Chest-to-wall Handstand Push-up

20 Muscle-up
